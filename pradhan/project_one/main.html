Hello, and welcome to Herbis Natura, your herb manufacturer from Berlin. We are pleased that you are interested in our company and that we have the opportunity to introduce ourselves to you.

About us

We are a young Berlin team for the nationwide shipping of natural herbs, medicinal plants, spices and teas from controlled organic cultivation. With a selection of hundreds of herbs, Herbis Natura is one of the largest online platforms for selling organic herbs in Germany. What started as a hobby and passion during our studies suddenly became a mammoth project and our main occupation today. We import our herbs from all over the world, taking into account the best organic quality and the European organic regulation. Every herb, every tea, every spice is lovingly bottled individually for you and leaves our house 1-2 days later, depending on the receipt of payment. In addition to the impeccable quality of our products, we offer you an equally good service and fast shipping directly to your home.

Certified quality

In order to ensure our high quality standards, we undergo regular and strict controls of the European organic regulation and thus, as a certified company, make our contribution to the excellent organic quality of our herbs. We also work exclusively with certified companies in order to be able to guarantee this standard throughout cultivation, through harvesting to processing.

Since, in our view, the basic idea of ​​organic goes beyond the quality of our herbs, we only work with recyclable packaging materials and bioplastics for the sake of the environment.

Our organic range

In addition to hundreds of individual herbs for teas and spices, we offer you a special selection of self-produced spice mixtures - from herbs de Provence , to Café de Paris or our Ayurveda spices such as breakfast stick kick, Agni Plus and vegetable spices - and of course every single ingredient from controlled organic cultivation . This is rounded off with a tasty range of tea blends from our own production. In addition to our classic organic teas, we offer you a selected selection of special tea blends for the palate and health, for well-being and relaxation.
